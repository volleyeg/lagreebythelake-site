The Method

It's not Pilates…
it's Lagree

A 50-minute full-body workout — tough on muscles, gentle on joints.

What is Lagree?

Strength. Cardio. Flexibility.
All in one.

Lagree by the Lake is a 50-minute full-body workout that focuses on muscular endurance, strength, and flexibility. It strengthens, tightens, and sculpts through slow and controlled movements.

While Lagree uses a machine with springs (similar to Reformer Pilates), the method incorporates strength training moves and body-building principles. We focus on slow, controlled movements and progressive load to build strength.

Trust us — if you like Pilates, you're going to love Lagree.

Prepare to shake, sweat, and feel the burn the following day!

Why it works

The best of every method

Str
Strength Training

Progressive load and body-building principles build real muscle. Slow, controlled movements under constant tension deliver fast, visible results.

Crd
Cardio

Quick transitions between blocks keep your heart rate elevated throughout the entire 50 minutes — no rest periods, maximum calorie burn.

Flx
Flexibility

The spring-based resistance system works with your body, not against it — improving range of motion and flexibility while you build strength.

The Machine

The Miniformer Pro

Classes are conducted on the Miniformer Pro — one of Lagree Fitness' latest machines. Don't let the compact size fool you.

"Don't worry, it packs a mega punch."

The spring-based resistance system allows for quick transitions between exercises while keeping muscles under constant tension — the key to the Lagree Method's results.

Suitable for all fitness levels. Our instructors offer modifications and progressions so every body gets the most out of every class.
