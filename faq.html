Before Class

Please arrive at least 10 minutes before class to allow time to select your machine, use the washroom, and get ready. We always give a demo of the machine and the leg block 5 minutes before class begins. If you have not taken a class at lagree by the lake before and miss the demo, we cannot let you stay for safety reasons.

Policies
Booking & Cancellations

To book a class, you will need an account and a valid credit balance. Click here to sign up. Drop-ins will not be accommodated, as waivers must be signed before class.

Our cancellation window is 12 hours before class to retain your credit. Please use your online account to cancel or reschedule classes. This window is designed to ensure that anyone who is waitlisted has the opportunity to join the class. If you cancel after 12 hours, your class credit will be deducted from your account.

If you have not taken a class at lagree by the lake before, you will not be permitted in class. If you have taken a class before, you will receive a 5-minute grace period. As this is a small-business studio, we do not have a front desk to monitor late check-ins. The studio door will be closed after the 5-minute mark — for your safety and to minimize disruptions for other clients in the zone. We understand life happens, and will re-credit your account as a one-time offer. Late cancellations after that will result in a credit deduction.
