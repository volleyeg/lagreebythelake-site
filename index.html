The Method

It's not Pilates…
it's Lagree

Lagree by the lake is a 50-minute full-body workout that focuses on muscular endurance, strength, and flexibility. It strengthens, tightens, and sculpts through slow and controlled movements. It's tough on muscles but gentle on joints.

Imagine the best parts of strength training, cardio and Pilates in one workout — that's the Lagree Method.

Prepare to shake, sweat, and feel the burn the following day! The class is conducted on one of Lagree Fitness' latest machines, the Miniformer Pro (don't worry, it packs a mega punch).

Looking for something to do in Muskoka this season? Lagree by the lake is Muskoka's only Lagree fitness studio, offering boutique workout classes designed for all fitness levels. Book a single class or grab a class pack — no membership, no commitment beyond what works for you.
