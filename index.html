The Method

It's not Pilates…
it's Lagree

Lagree by the Lake is a 50-minute full-body workout that focuses on muscular endurance, strength, and flexibility. Tough on muscles, gentle on joints.

Imagine the best parts of strength training, cardio, and Pilates combined in one workout. That's the Lagree Method — conducted on the Miniformer Pro.

Prepare to shake, sweat, and feel the burn the following day.
