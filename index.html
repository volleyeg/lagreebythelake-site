The Method

It's not Pilates…
it's Lagree

Lagree by the Lake is a 50-minute full-body workout that focuses on muscular endurance, strength, and flexibility. It strengthens, tightens, and sculpts through slow and controlled movements — tough on muscles, gentle on joints.

Imagine the best parts of strength training, cardio, and Pilates combined in one workout. That's the Lagree Method.

Classes are conducted on the Miniformer Pro — one of Lagree Fitness' latest machines. Don't let the size fool you; it packs a mega punch.

Prepare to shake, sweat, and feel the burn the following day.

New to Lagree?

For Your First Class

Everything you need to know before you walk through the door for the first time.

01
Grip Socks Required

Grip socks are mandatory for all classes for both safety and sanitary reasons. First-time visitors receive complimentary socks — after that, you can rent, purchase, or bring your own.

02
Arrive Early

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled class. This allows time to sign forms, receive your intro to the Miniformer Pro. We have a zero-minute late policy for new clients — if you miss the machine demo, we cannot let you join for safety reasons.

03
Listen & Have Fun

Lagree is about quick transitions between blocks to keep intensity high. Take breaks and hydrate when needed — you know your body best. We're up north for a good time. Leave the seriousness at the door!
