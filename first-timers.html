For Your First Class

Everything you need to know before you walk through the door.

What to expect

01

Grip Socks Are Required

Grip socks are mandatory for all classes — no exceptions. This is for sanitary and safety reasons. We offer complimentary socks to first-time visitors! After that, you can rent, purchase, or bring your own. If you are not wearing them, we will request that you rent or purchase them before class.

02

Arrive 10 Minutes Early

Please arrive at least 10 minutes before your scheduled class start time. This allows enough time to sign forms, purchase any add-ons (such as water or socks), and receive your introduction to the Miniformer Pro. As we're a small shop, we have a zero-minute late policy for new clients. If you did not receive a demo of the machine, we cannot let you in for safety reasons and will ask you to reschedule.

03

Listen to Your Body & Have Fun

While the moves are slow and controlled, Lagree is all about quick transitions between blocks to keep the intensity high. Take breaks and hydrate when needed — you know your body best. And last but not least, have fun. We're up north for a good time. Leave the seriousness at the door!

First Visit? Complimentary Socks on Us

We provide complimentary grip socks for all first-time visitors. After that, you can rent a pair, purchase a pair to keep, or bring your own.

Good to know

Studio Policies

Late Policy

If you have not taken a class at Lagree by the Lake before, you will not be permitted in class if you are late. If you have taken a class before, you receive a 5-minute grace period. The studio door will be closed after 5 minutes — for your safety and to minimize disruptions. We understand life happens and will re-credit your account as a one-time offer.

Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation window is 12 hours before class to retain your credit. Please use your online account to cancel or reschedule. This ensures anyone on the waitlist has the opportunity to join. If you cancel after 12 hours, your class credit will be deducted from your account.

Booking a Class

To book a class, you will need an account and a valid credit balance. Drop-ins will not be accommodated, as waivers must be signed before class. Credits available to purchase April 2026.

Pregnancy & Modifications

Yes! We offer modifications for pregnancy and post-pregnancy. Our instructors are trained to meet you where you are — modifications and progressions for all fitness levels. Please consult your doctor to ensure you are cleared for exercise.
